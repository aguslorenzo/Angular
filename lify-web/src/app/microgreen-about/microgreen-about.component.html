Lify was born out of a desire to meet a local need in the gastronomic sector of Tandil. This particular product was only available in a few other cities in the province, but due to logistical issues, it wasn't always a viable option. It is the venture of a group of friends curious to learn about indoor vegetable cultivation. Lify offers a wide range of microgreen varieties grown in a controlled, completely safe, and organic environment.
